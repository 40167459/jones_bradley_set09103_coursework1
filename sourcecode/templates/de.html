Arsenal: Demolition (de)

Demolition is a hybrid of the Bomb Scenario mode and the Gun Game mod. This is due to the player having no choice in what weapon they are using, as any players who made a (non-team) kill that round will recieve a new weapon in the next round, if a player eliminates more than one enemy in a round they will recieve highly valued equipment like the HE grenade. Players will continue to hold the equipment until they are killed. Weapons and equipment cannot be picked up after they are dropped. The round winning conditions are: Counter-Terrorists: Run the timer down without the bomb being planted. Defuse the C4 after it has been planted Eliminate all the Terrorists, provided they have not planted the bomb. Terrorists: Plant the C4 explosive, and prevent it from being disarmed. Eliminate all the Counter-Terrorists.
